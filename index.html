Working Towards Diversity, Inclusion and Belonging in Harvard SEAS

A CS171 Final Project by Jessica Edwards and Veronica Nutting

[placeholder text from our "Goals and Tasks"] For our visualization, our goal is to visualize data collected within SEASâ€™ Diversity, Inclusion, and Belonging (DIB) committee in order to analyze representation and retention data within the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at Harvard. Our group is very interested in issues relating to DIB, especially in regards to representation in technology. We are trying to display historical demographic breakdowns within concentrations, classes, and among different groups (undergraduates, graduate students, faculty, post-docs, etc.). We also aim to create a scalable project that could potentially be used on the SEAS website and updated as additional data is collected in future years.
