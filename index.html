Working Towards Diversity, Inclusion and Belonging in Harvard SEAS

A CS171 Final Project by Jessica Edwards and Veronica Nutting

This project aims to visualize data collected within SEAS’ Diversity, Inclusion, and Belonging (DIB) committee in order to analyze representation and retention data within the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at Harvard University. Understanding issues relating to DIB, especially in regards to representation in technology, is necessary for informed and effective policy-making. Displaying historical demographic breakdowns within concentrations, classes, and among different groups (undergraduates, graduate students, faculty, post-docs, etc.) can be useful for identifying trends and making sense of the current situation.
